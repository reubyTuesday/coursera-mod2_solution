Learning to Count

One
One is the beginning. It is pronounced won and it must be mastered before proceeding to two.
Two
Two is next. It is created through the merging of two ones. The ones must be complete on their own in order to create an adequate two.
Three
Three can sometimes be a bit of a surprise. It breaks the three letter word pattern of one and two. It may be difficult to get to three but it is often worth it.
